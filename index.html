All you need to know about Leopards

Basic Information

Leopards are big spotty jungle cats with pointed ears and long tails They are fast over short distances but not as fast as a Cheetah .

They are ferocious hunters, who stalk and hunt their prey. The types of prey they hunt are smaller animals such as gazelle
