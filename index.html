All you need to know about Leopards

Basic Information

Leopards are big spotty jungle cats with pointed ears and long tails. They are fast over short distances but not as fast as a Cheetah. and they live in sub-Saharan Africa, northeast Africa, Central Asia, India, and China with an average life span of 12 to 17 years. .

They are ferocious hunters, who stalk and hunt their prey. The types of prey they hunt are smaller animals such as a gazelle.

Although they are powerful in appearance, they are not that strong comapared to other carnivours. However its vast skill set make it a satisfactory hunter
